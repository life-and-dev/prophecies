# Fulfilled Prophecies

Christians present strong evidence that Jesus fulfilled the entire prophecy as originally described. These prophecies contain specific, verifiable details that match historical records of Jesus' life.

| Prophecy                                                                         | Reference            | Key Evidence                                                        |
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [The Prophet Like Moses](prophecies/messianic/prophet-like-moses.md)             | Deuteronomy 18:15-19 | Miraculous provision, covenant mediation, first-century recognition |
| [Not Abandoned to the Grave](prophecies/messianic/not-abandoned-to-grave.md)     | Psalm 16:10-11       | Peter's apostolic argument, resurrection before decomposition       |
| [Ascended to God's Right Hand](prophecies/messianic/ascended-gods-right-hand.md) | Psalm 110:1          | Multiple eyewitnesses, early church testimony in Jerusalem          |
