# Partially Fulfilled Prophecies

Only part of the prophecy has been fulfilled according to the original text. Christians argue the remaining portions will be fulfilled at Jesus' second coming due to the dual-nature of messianic prophecy (suffering servant at first coming, conquering king at second coming). 

Jews argue this indicates non-fulfillment since a true Messiah should fulfill all the prophecies to be validated as the Messiah.

| Prophecy                                                              | Reference     | Fulfilled                                | Awaiting Fulfillment                 |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------|------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| [The Beloved Servant of God](prophecies/messianic/beloved-servant.md) | Isaiah 42:1-4 | God's declarations in front of witnesses | He did not bring forth justice [yet] |
