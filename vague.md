# Vague Prophecies

Jesus could have fulfilled these prophecies, but they are either so general that they could apply to others, or the original context makes it unclear whether they are messianic predictions at all. These rely heavily on interpretation rather than specific details.

| Prophecy | Reference | Why Debatable |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| [No External Beauty](prophecies/messianic/no-external-beauty.md) | Isaiah 53:1-2 | Not specific enough to prove the Messiah's identity |
